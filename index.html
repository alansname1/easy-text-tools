What is a word counter?

A word counter tells you how many words are in your text. It is useful for essays, social posts, resumes, articles, and forms that have word limits.

What is reading time?

Reading time estimates how long a passage will take to read. This can help with blog writing, speeches, accessibility planning, and lesson preparation.

Why accessibility matters

Simple layouts, strong contrast, larger text, and clear buttons make online tools easier for seniors, disabled users, and anyone who prefers less clutter.
