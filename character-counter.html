Character Counter

This free character counter helps you count the number of characters in your text instantly. It can be useful for social media posts, online forms, writing assignments, and any text with character limits.

Some websites and applications limit how many characters you can use. This tool makes it easy to check your content before you submit it. You can use it for short posts, longer writing, and everyday text editing tasks.

Character counting is especially helpful when working with titles, descriptions, captions, and forms that require concise writing. It can also help improve clarity by encouraging shorter, more focused text.
